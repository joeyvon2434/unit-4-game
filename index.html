Star Wars: Starfighter Challenge!

Your goal is to defeat all your enemies.

1. Select your Starfighter. The other
Starfighters are your opponents.

2. Select your first opponent, then fire
with the 'Fire!' button.

3. Your prowess as a pilot grows with
experience, and your attack get stronger.

4. Once you defeat an opponent, you must
choose your next opponent.

5. Defeat all opponents to gain Victory!
